  •    How to
  • a. Use Configure option in side menu to manage your cases from majority of the High Courts or District courts or Both.
  • b. If you know the CNR Number of the Case, enter the 16 alphanumeric CNR Number without any – (hyphen) or space.
  • c. On Clicking the search button, the current status and entire history of the case will be shown.
  • d. If you don’t know the CNR number of the Case then it can be searched by other options like Case Registration Number, Party Name, Advocate Name etc. For this, click on the Case Status image
  • e. Click on Case Number to view current status, entire history and orders/judgments delivered in the case.
  • f. When you view the history of the case you have option to 'Save the Case' and can create your own portfolio of cases.
  • g. For Future reference, your saved cases can be seen in My Cases
