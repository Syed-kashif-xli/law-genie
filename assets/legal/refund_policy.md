# Cancellation & Refund Policy

**Last Updated: December 19, 2025**

Thank you for choosing Law Genie. We value your trust and aim to provide a transparent experience regarding your purchases.

## 1. Subscription Cancellations
- You can cancel your subscription at any time through the App settings or by contacting our support team.
- Upon cancellation, you will continue to have access to the premium features until the end of your current billing period.

## 2. Refund Eligibility
- Refunds are generally not provided for partially used subscription periods.
- In exceptional cases, such as technical failures that prevent access to the service for more than 48 hours, a pro-rata refund may be considered.
- Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the transaction.

## 3. Refund Process
- To request a refund, please email support@lawgenie.co.in with your transaction ID and the reason for the request.
- Once approved, the refund will be processed via Razorpay to your original payment method.
- It may take 5-7 working days for the refund to reflect in your account.

## 4. Duplicate Payments
In case of duplicate payments for the same subscription, the extra amount will be refunded automatically within 7-10 working days after verification.

## 5. Contact Information
For any questions related to refunds or cancellations, please reach out to us:
**Email:** support@lawgenie.co.in
