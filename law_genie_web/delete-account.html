Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Law Genie account and permanently remove your data, you can do so by contacting our support team or using the in-app settings. Please note that this action is irreversible.

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Law Genie App.
  2. Go to Settings > Profile.
  3. Scroll down and tap on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the action to permanently delete your data.

Method 2: Email Request

You can also request account deletion by sending an email to our support team:

  • Email: support@lawgenie.co.in
  • Subject: Request to Delete Account
  • Please include your registered email address or phone number in the email body for verification.

What Information will be Deleted?

Upon deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Personal profile information (Name, Email, Phone).
  • Authentication credentials.
  • All chat history and saved legal queries.
  • Uploaded documents and case files.

Note: Payment transaction logs may be retained for accounting and legal compliance purposes as required by law.
